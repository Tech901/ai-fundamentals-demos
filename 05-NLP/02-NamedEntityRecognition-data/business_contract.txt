MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026, by and between:

APERTURE LABORATORIES, INC., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 1500 Innovation Drive, Cleveland, Ohio 44113 ("Provider"), and

UMBRELLA CORPORATION, a multinational conglomerate incorporated in the State of New York with headquarters at 2800 Corporate Plaza, Suite 500, Raccoon City, New York 10001 ("Client").

WHEREAS, Provider specializes in advanced machine learning solutions, artificial intelligence research, and data analytics services; and

WHEREAS, Client desires to engage Provider to deliver machine learning consulting services, model development, and AI infrastructure support;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set forth, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1 Provider agrees to deliver the following services to Client:
   (a) Development and deployment of custom machine learning models for pharmaceutical research optimization
   (b) Natural language processing solutions for clinical trial documentation
   (c) Predictive analytics platforms for supply chain management
   (d) AI-powered quality control systems for manufacturing facilities

1.2 Services shall be performed at Client's facilities located in Raccoon City, New York, and remotely from Provider's headquarters in Cleveland, Ohio.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2026, and continue through January 31, 2029 (the "Initial Term").

2.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with ninety (90) days written notice to the other party.

3. COMPENSATION

3.1 Client agrees to pay Provider the following fees:
   (a) Initial setup and integration: $2,500,000 USD
   (b) Monthly retainer for ongoing services: $175,000 USD
   (c) Additional development work: $350 USD per hour

3.2 All invoices shall be paid within thirty (30) days of receipt.

4. KEY PERSONNEL

4.1 Provider designates the following individuals as key personnel for this engagement:
   - Dr. Caroline Johnson, Chief AI Officer
   - Marcus Chen, Director of Machine Learning Operations
   - Dr. Elena Rodriguez, Lead Data Scientist

4.2 Client designates the following individuals as primary contacts:
   - William Birkin, VP of Research and Development
   - Dr. Annette Thompson, Director of Information Technology
   - Albert Wesker, Chief Operations Officer

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 All intellectual property developed specifically for Client under this Agreement shall be owned by Client upon full payment.

5.2 Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property, tools, and methodologies.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information exchanged during the course of this engagement.

7. GOVERNING LAW

7.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
